Soapberri

Building a website with no brand.
About

Soapberri Co. comes into the cleaning industry to disrupt our whole concept of soap. The soapberry literally comes from a tree, rub it in your hands, and out comes anti-microbial, hypoallergenic soap.

Challenge

With no experience at hand, I needed to create a website from thin air. The Soapberri team had no branding, research, or target market, so the design process was constantly changing.

Role

I threw my ideas down on branding, guerilla marketing, social media-everything. Starting from ground zero and leading up to Kickstarter's most popular Health & Beauty campaign.
